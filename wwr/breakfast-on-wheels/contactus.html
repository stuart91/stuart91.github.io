Why visit Us?

The main reason why people will come to my website is to see the different types of amazing foods that Breakfast on Wheels has to offer. We will have the majority of quick, on the go breakfast foods available in under a minute. We want to keep things quick because of how busy students are. If they are waiting longer than 1-2 minutes, then they are waiting too long.

Breakfast on Wheels does not stay in one consistent place. We will have a schedule of where we will be throughout the week. We will mostly be around heavier populated areas around the morning rush hour. Local college campuses in particular. These locations can change frequently, or where we are wanted most. If someone has a location they would like to recommend us, they are more than welcome to message us in our recommend box!
