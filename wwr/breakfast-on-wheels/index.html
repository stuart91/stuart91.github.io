Why Visit Us?

The main reasoning behind people from all over town come and visit Breakfast on Wheels is to see the different types of amazing goods that we have to offer. The advantage Breakfast on Wheels has over other fast-food restaurants is that we are mobile. Having our restaurant in a food truck means the ability to take our food to our customers. We change our locations throughout the week and are usually near construction sites and college campuses. We sell breakfast foods that are quick to make and quick to eat. We believe if our customers must wait longer than 2 minutes for their breakfast, then they are waiting too long.

Having a guarantee of providing quick, on-the-go foods means foods such as Pop Tarts, bagels with spreads, breakfast bars, yogurts, granola, and healthier options such as apples, bananas, and other different fruits. If they have a few minutes to spare before class, we have an assortment of cereals with different types of milk to choose from. Providing a quick breakfast for them means providing the fuel and energy that students need to help excel in their studies and to better their future!
