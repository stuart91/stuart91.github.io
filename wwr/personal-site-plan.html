Overview

Purpose

With Breakfast on Wheels, we provide a quick breakfast for students who don't have the time to eat breakfast at home. Providing breakfast on campus means students have the fuel and energy needed to help excel in their studies and to better their future.

Audience

Studies show that 60% of students skip breakfast. The meal that has been proven to be the most important meal of the day. When time gets the best of students, they choose to go without breakfast. A result that can cause students to struggle with the concentration they need while in school. With “Breakfast on Wheels”, we take the breakfast to students on campus. Being the breakfast of taco trucks means quick, on-the-go foods such as Pop Tarts, bagels with spreads, breakfast bars, yogurts, granola, and healthier options such as apples, bananas, and other different fruits. If they have a few minutes to spare before class, we have an assortment of cereals with different types of milk to choose from.

Home page

Why people come to this site.

The main reason why people will come to my website is to see the different types of amazing foods that Breakfast on Wheels has to offer. We will have the majority of quick, on the go breakfast foods available in under a minute. We want to keep things quick because of how busy students are. If they are waiting longer than 1-2 minutes, then they are waiting too long. Breakfast on Wheels does not stay in one consistent place. We will have a schedule of where we will be throughout the week. We will mostly be around heavier populated areas around the morning rush hour. Local college campuses in particular. These locations can change frequently, or where we are wanted most. If someone has a location they would like to recommend us, they are more than welcome to message us in our recommend box!
